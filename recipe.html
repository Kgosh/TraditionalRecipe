Taste Of The Heritage;mopani worms

Savor the flavour of Southern Africa:Authentic Mopani worms - Indulge in a protein rich dish , cooked to bring out theit goodness. This dish is cooked to perfection using vegetables and seasonings

Prep Time

  • Total : -30 minutes
  • Preparation : +5 minutes
  • cooking : +15 minutes

Ingridients

  • 1 cup of Mopani Worms
  • 1 large / 2 medium sized tomatos
  • 1 onion
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Preffered seasonings(optional)
  • Peri-peri(optional)

Instructions

1.

Soak the worms: In a bowl or container soak the worms with hot water for 5 minutes

2.

chop your vegies: Dice your onion and tomato . Put the in different bowls.

3.

Heat the pan ot pot: Place the pan or pot over medium heat and pour 4 tablespoons of oil or enough to fry your oinion

4.

Add your vegies: Fry your onions until translusant or to your desired point then add tomatos and cook until soft then mash like until it looks like gravy

5.

Add your seasonings: Add salt according to your taste buds and Preffered spice and Peri-peri

6.

Add Mopani Worm: Remove mopani worms from hot water and add in the pot or pan the mix with the tomatoes and onion ( make sure you mix well)

7.

Add water: Add 150 ml water and on low medium heat let it simmer for 3-5 minutes. Do not let burn. Dish on a plate

6.

Devour: Serve hot , with pap and feast on BUON APPETITO
